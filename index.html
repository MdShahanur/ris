About Us

Welcome to Shaheed Salahuddin Cantonment Internet Service (SSCIS), a non-profit Internet Service Provider operating under "3 Signal Battalion," providing high-quality and secure internet services to the military personals of Shaheed Salahuddin Cantonment, 19 Infantry Division located in Ghatail, Tangail, Bangladesh.

At SSCIS, we take pride in being a trusted internet service provider to our esteemed clients. Our aim is to provide uninterrupted internet services that are fast, reliable, and secure. We understand the importance of staying connected, and therefore, we use the latest technology to offer the best possible internet services to our clients.

We have a team of expert technicians who work hard to ensure that our clients receive the best possible internet experience. Our 24/7 support team is always available to resolve any issues that our clients may face.

As a military ISP, security is our topmost priority, and we ensure that all our clients' data is protected from any unauthorized access. We use advanced security protocols to keep our clients' data secure and confidential.

Our mission is to provide the best possible internet services to the Officers, JCO, and Soldiers of the cantonment. We are committed to ensuring that our clients receive the best internet services that meet their needs and exceed their expectations.

Join us and experience the difference in internet services. We are dedicated to providing our clients with high-quality and secure internet services that they can rely on.

The Shaheed Salahuddin Cantonment Internet Service ("SSCIS") Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2023

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully and contact us at info@sscisp.com if you have any questions. This privacy policy sets out how SSCIS collects, uses, discloses, stores and protects any information that you provide when you subscribe to use our internet service(s). SSCIS is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and we do not share any of your information with any other third parties except where required by law.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

What personal information we collect and how we use it are listed here:

- When you subscribe to SSCIS services, you may have to provide us with some of your personal information, including your name, address, email address, phone number, date of birth, National Identity Number, and your passport size photograph. We may collect your location information and device identity to identify you concisely to provide our services.
- When you access our internet services, we provide an authentication number along with your user ID and other information that is saved to your device to let you use SSCIS securely for the entire session, and during logout, we erase all data that we provided during login.
- During your monthly bill payment, our payment gateways require your debit/credit card information or your mobile banking information to successfully complete online bill payment.
